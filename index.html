About Us

A sanctuary for beautiful braids

Binda Beauty SZ is a braiding studio nestled in the heart of Manzini, Eswatini. We specialise exclusively in braiding,from elegant knotless and box braids to detailed cornrows, twists, and protective styles.

Every appointment is a personal experience,we use premium hair, gentle techniques, and take the time your hair deserves.

  • Specialist braiders only
  • Premium quality hair & products
  • Hygienic, relaxing environment
  • Personalised consultations

Salon Policies

What you should know

Booking Policy

Booking Requirements All appointments must be booked at least 24 hours in advance. Last-minute and same-day appointments will not be accepted. Verbal bookings are not allowed. A 50% non-refundable deposit secures your appointment.Your appointment is not confirmed until the deposit has been received.

Cancellation Policy

We do understand that sometimes schedule adjustments are necessary; Therefore, we respectfully request at least immediate notice for cancellation Failure to show up at your scheduled time, will lead to your appointment being canceled immediately.The deposit is non- refundable, and no exceptions will be made. We do not reschedule appointments.If you miss your appointment, you will need to book a new one with a new deposit. By booking an appointment, you acknowledge and agree to these terms. THANK YOU.

Health & Safety

All tools are sanitised between clients. We work in a clean, hygienic environment and ask that clients let us know of any scalp sensitivities.

Late Policy

OF COURSE, LIFE HAPPENS - WE GET IT. IF YOU ARE RUNNING LATE PLEASE CALL AND INFORM US. HOWEVER, WE GENERALLY ALLOW 20MIN GRACE PERIOD WITH A E35 LATE COMING FEE 45MIN LATE WILL RESULT IN YOUR APPOINTMENT BEING CANCELLED.

Privacy Policy

Your personal details are kept confidential and used only for booking. Client photos are shared on social media only with your permission.

Children & Guests

To maintain a relaxing atmosphere, please limit extra guests. Children should only attend if they have their own appointment.
